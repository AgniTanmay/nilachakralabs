What we do (in plain words)

We turn website activity into clear, usable guidance for your team. Instead of long reports with numbers you don’t use, we give you simple recommendations: who to contact, what pages to fix, and when to run campaigns to get more students or visitors.

Understand who visits:
We group visitors into meaningful categories — for example: likely applicants, casual browsers, repeat visitors — so you know where to focus recruitment effort.
Improve conversion (more enquiries):
We predict which visitors are most likely to take action (inquire or apply) and suggest small website or messaging changes that increase responses.
Plan better timing:
We forecast when interest will rise (e.g., before admission deadlines or events) so you can schedule outreach at the right moment.

Why this matters

Traditional analytics show "what happened." This system answers "who is likely to convert," "when interest will peak," and "what content to prioritize" — turning analytics into a strategic decision- support tool for universities.

Typical results our clients see

  • Clear prioritization of high-potential leads — contact these first.
  • Improved webpage layouts that increase form submissions.
  • Better timing of campaigns, increasing enquiries around deadlines.
